This event rewards are one time only and can be done at any time without any loss.

<Callout type="default">
	You can read more about Tower of Babyl
	[here](https://gbf.wiki/Tower_of_Babyl)
</Callout>

## How to play this event?

You clear the quests and fulfill the missions to get rewards.
If you can complete every mission in one area, then you get additional rewards.

<Callout type="warning">
	This event will want you to have relatively diverse
	character pool. If you are stuck in one quest/floor, you
	can come back later when the even reruns.
</Callout>
