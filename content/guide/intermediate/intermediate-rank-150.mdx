### Hollowsky Weapons

These weapons are the first weapons to have supplemental skill in the game.
However they have annoying conditions attached and has become mostly obsolete

This raid drops a `Gold Brick` in the Blue Chest.
Materials from this raid is used to further upgrade Cosmic Weapons

### Cosmos Weapons

<Callout type="default">
    Cosmos weapons are rarely used so no rush to farm it.
</Callout>

These weapons can actually be redeemed with Grand Order Normal materials,
however the weapon at that stage is not good enough to be mentioned.

Cosmos weapons are unique in that they boost the stats of other weapons in the grid,
making this weapon quite universal.

<Callout type="default">
    You can read more about Cosmos Weapons
    [here](https://gbf.wiki/Cosmos_Weapons)
</Callout>

## Beelzebub

<div className="my-6 w-full overflow-y-auto">
    <Image
        src="/images/impossible/beelzebub.jpg"
        alt="Beelzebub"
        height={0}

</div>

This raid is another **6 man** raid however unlike UBHL, does not drop significant rewards.
You can trade materials from this raid for Astral Weapons in `Shop > Treasure Trade > Weapons > Astral Weapons`.

### Astral Weapons

These weapons have Unworldly EX skills and also provide Cap Up like Ultima / Dark Opus's 2nd Skill.

<Callout type="warning">
    The materials needed to redeem the weapons are too
    expensive for newer players such that we recommend newer
    players to use Xeno Weapons instead.
</Callout>

<Callout type="default">
    You can read more about Astral Weapons
    [here](https://gbf.wiki/Astral_Weapons)
</Callout>
