### Free Quest

You are able to host the free quest version twice daily.
This can be a good daily source of Quartz, Scale and 6 Dragon Advent Mats.

### Raid

The raid is pretty popular as it drops [Earrings]() and [Eternity Sand]().

    The materials from the 6 Dragons can be used to further upgrade Astral and Draconic Weapon into 5* / ULB.

### Ancestral Weapons

These weapons are really good as it brought more supplemental.
