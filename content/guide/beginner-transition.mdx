Upon reaching Rank 120, the game opens up with a lot more content.

Congratulations on finishing the tutorial, welcome to the starting point.

## Whats in Intermediate

These will be touched upon again on the Intermediate Section

### Magna 2 (Regalia)

Another Magna squad appears before you to upgrade your grid.

    These are unlocked at **Rank 120**. These raids should not be leeched as their weapons only drop from Blue Chests.

### Malice / Menace

These raids drop pretty unique weapon but fortunately you only need to farm selected Malice raids as the others are just not good

    These are unlocked at **Rank 120**. These raids can be leeched and has no blue chest

### 4 Primarch

The Primarchs has assembled into one, giving you the chance to farm for all 4 elements (Fire, Water, Earth, Wind) in one place.
They drop materials to further upgrade your Seraphic Weapon

    These are unlocked at **Rank 120**.

### Lucilius (ExternalLinkIcon)

    His nickname is Faa

The raid that is often called LuciN or FaaN even though its an Impossible Raid as he has a harder version which we
call LuciHL / FaaHL. He drops material for Dark Opus Weapon.

    These are unlocked at **Rank 120**.
